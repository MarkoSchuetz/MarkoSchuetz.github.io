A smoothie characterized by the use of celery and peanut butter.

Recently, I started making smoothies for a change. So now I am experimenting with all kinds of combinations of fruits, cereals, vegetables, … Often it boils down to scavenging the fridge to see what's there…

1 Ingredients

  • 1 ripe d'Anjou pear
  • 1 very ripe banana
  • generous amount of red grapes
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 1 heaped tbsp of unsweetened peanut butter
  • 16 oz of homemade kefir
  • 6 tbsp of homemade muesli mix (quick oats, traditional oats, almonds, walnuts, coconut, sesame seeds, cocoa nips)

2 Method

  • put ingredients in blender
  • blend on "XL smoothie" program
